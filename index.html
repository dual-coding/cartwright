At Cartwright Comedy, we strive to bring you the best comedians.

That's why we are proud to be featuring Brendan D'Souza as our headliner!

Brendan has been featured on CBC and CTV, most notably on Roast Battle Canada. Brendan has also performed at Just For Laughs, the largest comedy festival in the world.

By attending the show, one thing's for sure – you'll be watching a proven comedic talent!

The show will take place at Salto Restaurant & Bar, a charming Italian bistro located just moments away from downtown Toronto. They offer some of the best pizza and pasta in the city at prices you can afford!

Tickets are $8. When you buy 2 or more tickets, tickets are only $5!
